CE METAL FABRICATION AT A GLANCE

Christiansen Enterprises (CE) began as a custom dry kiln fabricator for the lumber industry. We are always inspired by a challenge and enjoy the pursuit of our customer’s happiness. So, we expanded into custom metal works, forming CE Metal Fabrication.

Our team has more than 100 years combined experience in drafting, cutting, forming and welding, and we’re committed to exactness in every craft.

OUR PROMISE

While we continue to work with our friends in the lumber industry, we also serve customers in commercial, structural, and even automotive industries for projects big and small. We understand that no matter the project, time and money are critical, which is why you can always count on us to complete your projects on schedule and within budget. You can also expect us to treat your project with care and consideration– that’s our promise to you.

We create accurate, long-lasting structures and assemblies ideally suited for your custom use and built to the highest standards.

We are experienced creating custom staircases, high-end stainless handrails, and other commercial building components. But we’re not just a fab shop. We’re also fully licensed, bonded, and insured for industrial field work across the Pacific Northwest. Our AWS & WABO Certified Welders can handle anything you throw at them.
