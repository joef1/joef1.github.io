WELCOME TO REGAL RESEARCH & MFG. CO.

We are a complete in-house contract manufacturing solution for mechanical products, assemblies, and parts that are made in the USA. With comprehensive in-house manufacturing capabilities including sheet metal fabrication, CNC machining, welding, plating, wet and powder painting, electrical and mechanical assembly, and design engineering support, Regal can cost effectively manufacture your products with the highest quality and unparalleled customer service. We are centrally located in the heart of Texas with over 40 years experience and a highly-skilled management team and workforce of over 200 persons. Our customers include many Fortune 500 OEMs in the medical, defense, semiconductor equipment, aerospace, and industrial markets. We invite you to experience the Regal Research difference and see why so many companies have chosen us as their supply chain consolidation solution and manufacturing partner.

2016 Regal Research and Mfg. Co.

Regal Research and Mfg. Co. is actively producing parts and assemblies for our customers whose products are life-saving medical equipment which are used in the fight against COVID-19 and other diseases. Everyone at Regal is making a difference by being part of this effort and also by following all the precautions to stay healthy so we can manufacture this critical equipment.

Although what we are doing is extremely important right now, keeping our employees and team members safe is our number one priority during this global event. As such, Regal Research has formed an internal task force that is actively engaged in educating our team, enforcing newly developed policies and enhancing our COVID-19 responses. Our goal is to continue production without interruption while maintaining the same output volume in keeping with our customers’ requirements. We have enacted tighter controls on those entering our facilities, developed work from home programs for certain functions, enhanced our cleaning and disinfecting of common areas, and developed a response policy in the event that any of our team or their family comes in contact with, or is infected by, a person testing positive with the COVID-19 virus.

 

If you have any questions regarding our COVID-19 response or production capacity, please reach out to Brian Imbler (VP of Mfg and Business Development) at 972-494-0359.
